# Direct Source Watchlist

Use this file when you want direct content-entry pages that can often be fetched more reliably than broad search results.

Treat these pages as starting points. They are useful for discovery and direct extraction, but still verify dates, deduplicate before output, and pair them with targeted search-engine queries.

## How to Use This Watchlist

For a broad daily scan:

1. Pull at least one regulator page.
2. Pull at least one platform page.
3. Pull at least two professional restaurant-media pages.
4. Pull at least one property or channel page.
5. Pull at least one business or local-services media page.
6. Run search-engine queries across at least the regulator, platform, and brand/company buckets during the same run.

Recommended daily base set:

1. Restaurant Boss Insider
2. Red Meal
3. Canyinjie
4. Jiemian
5. Winshang
6. Ebrun
7. one regulator source
8. one platform-official source
9. the Tier 1 brand-official set from `brand-official-watchlist.md`

Recommended brand routine:

1. always check the Tier 1 brand set
2. rotate two or three Tier 2 brands into each broad daily scan
3. immediately promote a rotating brand if it appears elsewhere in the day's media, regulator, platform, or property signals

For slow news days:

- Keep the scan narrow and fresh.
- Return fewer items rather than padding with stale material.
- Move weak or unconfirmed items into a watchlist section.
- Even on slow days, keep at least one targeted search-engine pass so the run does not collapse into direct-page-only fetching.

## Maintenance Notes

- Mobile or lightweight list pages are often easier to fetch than desktop homepages.
- Some pages may rely on JavaScript or bot protection and can change over time.
- If a direct page degrades, keep the bucket but replace the entry point with a fresher source rather than removing the bucket.
