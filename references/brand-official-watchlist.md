# Brand Official Watchlist

Use this file when you want a stable set of official brand sources that can be checked in every broad scan or on a scheduled basis.

The goal is not to watch every restaurant brand. The goal is to track a representative top set across coffee, tea drinks, quick service, hotpot, and chain dining.

## Monitoring Rules

- Check these brands for major product launches, store openings, market entry, official responses, financing, earnings, or franchise-policy changes.
- Prefer the official site, newsroom, or investor-relations page first.
- If the official site is thin, use the corporate or investor page as the confirmation source and let trade media act as the discovery layer.
- Do not treat a reposted franchise site as the official source unless the brand itself clearly identifies it as the only official site.

## Daily Monitoring Tiers

### Tier 1: Must-watch every broad daily scan

Use these five brands in nearly every broad daily scan because they are high-signal across coffee, tea, hotpot, and chain dining:

- Luckin Coffee
- Mixue
- Guming
- Haidilao
- Nayuki

Why these five:

- they are large enough to create category signals rather than isolated store news
- they frequently surface pricing, product, expansion, or capital-market signals
- together they cover coffee, tea drinks, hotpot, and listed chain dynamics

### Tier 2: Rotating watchlist

Use these five on a rotating basis, or promote them when the day's media coverage shows stronger activity:

- Chagee
- Hushang Ayi
- Xibei
- Laoxiangji
- Chabaidao

Recommended rotation options:

- rotate two or three Tier 2 brands into each broad daily scan
- rotate by category so the scan does not overfit one sub-sector
- immediately promote a Tier 2 brand into active tracking if that brand appears in trade media, regulator notices, platform changes, or mall-entry reporting

## What Counts As A Brand-Level Trigger

- official new-product launch
- flagship or first-city opening
- official response to controversy
- regulator issue tied to the brand
- franchise-policy update
- earnings, financing, or listing event
- overseas market entry

## Daily Execution Suggestion

For a normal broad daily run:

1. Check all Tier 1 brands
2. Add two or three Tier 2 brands on rotation
3. Promote any Tier 2 brand immediately if it appears elsewhere in the day's regulator, platform, trade-media, or property signals
